Spoilers Ahead

spoiler alert

The trailer itself is action-packed while giving away almost no spoilers. Rey, the woman raiding the star destroyer was in the trailer and was one of the main characters in the movie. The start of another Jedi got fans excited to watch the movie. Kylo Ren was in the trailer too, but without major spoilers. The only spoiler is when Kylo Ren used the force on someone.

The crashed Star Destroyer

The crashed star destroyer gives fans a look at how big a ship is compared to a human. THeorists can now correctly scale a human to any ship or station in Star Wars.
